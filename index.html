French Club

About our club

French Club is all about appreciating all things French! We meet in room 116 on Tuesdays at 4:00pm. Be sure to follow us on Instagram at @cdo_clubdefrancais to see pictures and videos of what we've been up to and for updates.

How to join

Joining French Club is easy! Simpy send an email to cdofrenchclub@gmail.com to let our club sponsor that you are interested. They will give you the information about club dues and other formalities. We can't wait for you to join us!

Upcoming events

At our most recent meetings, we planned our semi-annual picnic, and a Mardi Gras themed movie night. At our movie night, we'll be watching La Princesse et la Grenouille while enjoying beignets and lemonade!
